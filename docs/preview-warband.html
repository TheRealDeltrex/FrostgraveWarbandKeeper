General Arms and Armour List (2e). Spellcasters may not wear armour or carry shields.

Item Category Summary
Dagger Weapon −1 damage. First dagger does not take an item slot. Info

A knife or other small weapon such as a light club. Daggers have a −1 damage modifier. The first dagger carried by a figure does not take up an item slot.

Hand Weapon Weapon No combat modifiers. One item slot. Info

Swords, scimitars, hand axes, maces, light spears, etc. No modifiers in combat.

Two-Handed Weapon Weapon +2 damage. Takes two item slots. Info

Two-handed swords, battle-axes, polearms, large flails, heavy spears. +2 damage. Takes two item slots.

Staff Weapon −1 damage; opponent −1 damage in hand-to-hand. Info

Staffs do −1 damage and give the opponent −1 damage in hand-to-hand. Cannot be combined with a shield.

Bow Missile Range 24". Load and fire as one action. Needs a quiver. Info

Load and fire as a single action. Maximum range 24". Requires a quiver.

Crossbow Missile +2 damage, range 24". Separate load and fire. Needs a quiver. Info

One action to load and one to fire. +2 damage, range 24". Requires a quiver.

Quiver Gear Required for bow/crossbow. One free magic ammo slot. Info

Required to shoot a bow or crossbow. May carry one magic ammunition without an extra slot.

Shield Armour +1 Armour. Not with two-handed weapon or staff. No spellcasters. Info

+1 Armour. Cannot be carried with a two-handed weapon or staff. Spellcasters may not carry shields.

Light Armour Armour +1 Armour. No spellcasters. Info

+1 Armour. Spellcasters may not wear light armour.

Heavy Armour Armour +2 Armour, −1 Move. No spellcasters. Info

+2 Armour but −1 Move. Spellcasters may not wear heavy armour.

Spells (wizard & apprentice)

Difficulty is shown as Wizard CN / Apprentice CN (apprentice has −2 to cast rolls, so +2 effective difficulty). CN already includes school penalty (own +0, aligned +2, neutral +4, opposed +6). Open Info on a spell line to read what it does.

# Spell Rel. School Wiz/App Type
1. Wall own Elementalist 10/12 Line of Sight Info

This spell creates a 6”-long, 3”-high wall, part of which must be within 10” and line of sight of the spellcaster. This wall can be climbed as normal. At the end of each turn, after the turn in which the spell was cast, roll a die, on a 1–4 the wall vanishes. ELEMENTALIST

2. Elemental Bolt own Elementalist 12/14 Line of Sight Info

The spellcaster makes a +7 elemental magic shooting attack against a target figure within 16” and line of sight.

3. Elemental Shield own Elementalist 10/12 Self Only Info

The spellcaster forms a floating shield that absorbs the next 3 points of damage the spellcaster would normally suffer in combat or from a shooting attack. Once 3 points have been absorbed the spell is cancelled. A spellcaster may only have one Elemental Shield active at any time.

4. Fast Act aligned Chronomancer 10/12 Line of Sight Info

This spell may only be cast on a member of the spellcaster’s warband or an uncontrolled creature. This figure will activate at the end of the current phase instead of in its normal phase. For example, if an apprentice casts this spell on an uncontrolled creature, the creature will activate at the end of that player’s Apprentice phase instead of the Creature phase. Spellcasters may not cast this spell on themselves, nor on a figure that has already activated in the current turn.

5. Enchant Weapon aligned Enchanter 10/12 Line of Sight Info

This spell targets a weapon of the spellcaster’s choosing. If cast on a melee weapon, this weapon counts as a magic weapon with +1 Fight. Bows and crossbows count as magic weapons with +1 Shoot, but the attacks made with them do not count as magic attacks. This spell may be cast on a single arrow or crossbow bolt, in which case that ammunition gives +1 Shoot and its attack counts as magic, but for the next shooting attack only. This spell may only be cast once on each weapon. When using both a magic missile weapon and magic ammunition, the shooter may choose to apply the bonus of one or the other, but not both.

6. Leap aligned Summoner 10/12 Line of Sight Info

This spell may only be cast on a member of the spellcaster’s warband. Immediately move the target figure up to 10” in any direction, including vertically. This move must either be in a straight line or an arc. It cannot curve around corners. If this move leaves the figure above the ground, it immediately falls, taking damage as normal. If the target is carrying treasure, this move is reduced to 5”. This move may not take a figure off the table or into combat. The target of the Leap spell may take no other actions this turn, though it may have taken actions previously.

7. Bone Dart neutral Necromancer 14/16 Line of Sight Info

This spell fires a small, sharp shard of bone. The spellcaster makes a +5 shooting attack against any figure within line of sight and 12”. This does not count as a magic attack.

8. Heal neutral Thaumaturge 12/14 Line of Sight Info

This spell restores up to 5 points of lost Health to a target figure within 6”. This spell cannot take a model above its starting Health. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
