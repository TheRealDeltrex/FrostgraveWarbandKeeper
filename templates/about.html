About

Local multi-page Frostgrave warband creator and campaign tracker. No login; data stays on your PC.

Features

  • Wizard: name, school, portrait, 8 spells with CN
  • Apprentice: name + portrait; stats/spells auto from wizard
  • Level-up wizard (stats / learn / improve spell); apprentice auto-levels
  • Recruit soldiers with cost + total/specialist limits
  • Post-game gold, XP, and items into treasury/vault
  • PDF roster: wizard, apprentice, spells, soldiers
  • Name + picture on every character

Data & disclaimer

Warbands: data/warbands/ · Portraits: data/portraits/

Unofficial fan tool — not affiliated with Osprey Games or Joseph A. McCullough. Spell casting numbers follow 2e spell cards; always check your rulebook.
